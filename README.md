💳 Mitron Bank Credit Card Strategy Analysis
📌 Project Overview

Mitron Bank, a legacy financial institution headquartered in Hyderabad, aims to launch a new line of credit cards to expand its product portfolio and strengthen its competitive position in the financial services market.

This project leverages data analytics to understand customer demographics, spending behavior, and credit utilization patterns, enabling data-driven decision-making for product design, targeting, and strategic planning.

🎯 Project Objectives
Customer Demographics Analysis
Analyze customer distribution based on age, gender, occupation, and geography.
Spending & Income Insights
Study income levels and spending patterns to align credit card features with customer lifestyles.
Customer Segmentation
Identify high-value and high-potential customer groups.
Insights & Recommendations
Deliver actionable insights through dashboards and reports for business decision-making.
📂 Dataset Description
1. Dim_Customers

A dimension table containing customer-level information:

Demographics (age, gender)
Occupation
Income group
Geographic data
2. Fact_Spends

A fact table capturing transaction-level data:

Customer ID
Transaction date
Spending category
Amount spent

💡 Recommendations
1. 🎯 Primary Target Segment

Urban Salaried Professionals (Age 25–45)
Cities: Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi NCR

Recommended Product: Lifestyle Rewards Credit Card

Cashback on groceries, bills, online shopping
Travel & dining rewards
EMI options
Annual fee waiver on threshold spend
2. 💼 Secondary Segment

Freelancers & Self-Employed

Recommended Product: Flexible Spending Credit Card

Higher credit limits
Flexible billing cycles
Rewards on digital payments
Instant credit limit enhancements
3. 🛡️ Low-Risk Segment

Government Employees

Recommended Product: Low-Interest Credit Card

Lower interest rates
Conservative credit limits
Minimal annual fees
Focus on long-term relationship
4. 👩 Women-Focused Opportunity

Strategy: Women-Centric Credit Card

Rewards on wellness, shopping, education
Higher reward multipliers
Travel benefits
5. 🎯 Age-Based Targeting Strategy
Age Group	Recommendation
21–25	Starter Card (low limits, cashback, credit building)
26–35	Lifestyle Rewards Card (cashback, travel, EMI benefits)
36–45	Premium Card (higher limits, family benefits)
46–60	Low-interest/Premium Card (security & wellness perks)
